About MyFigPoint

Our Company

MyFigPoint is an independent loyalty and distributor-reward platform designed to help users earn, track, and redeem reward points from participating companies across Africa.

We empower brands to appreciate loyalty, motivate performance, and reward genuine engagement through a simple and transparent digital system.

Our Mission:

To build Africa's most trusted digital reward ecosystem

Policies & Information

We operate with full transparency in our reward processing and payout policies. MyFigPoint acts as a facilitator between users and brands.

All rewards are fulfilled directly by the issuing brand, not by MyFigPoint.

Key Policy:

Points once redeemed cannot be reversed or refunded
