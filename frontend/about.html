Official Company Profile & Reward Policy

Date: October 8, 2025 • Operated by BlueTech Resources Ltd

ABOUT MYFIGPOINT

MyFigPoint is an independent loyalty and distributor-reward platform designed to help users earn, track, and redeem reward points from participating companies across Africa.

We empower brands to appreciate loyalty, motivate performance, and reward genuine engagement — all through a simple and transparent digital system.

Unlike traditional systems, MyFigPoint does not handle or transfer Reward directly. Instead, it provides a secure platform where users can:

  • View their accumulated points
  • Request product or cash-equivalent rewards
  • Track redemption progress

Our Mission

To build Africa’s most trusted digital reward ecosystem that inspires loyalty, promotes transparency, and connects brands with their customers through purpose-driven incentives.

Our Vision

To empower individuals to turn passion into purpose through digital recognition, loyalty, and reward innovation.

REWARD POLICY

1. Purpose

This policy outlines how MyFigPoint processes reward redemptions and communicates payout requests to partner companies. It ensures transparency between users, brands, and the platform.

2. Nature of Service

MyFigPoint is a reward-management and communication system, not a financial institution. It allows users to accumulate points from verified brands and submit redemption requests for rewards. MyFigPoint itself does not handle, hold, or transfer any funds on behalf of users or companies.

3. Redemption Process

Users submit a redemption request via their dashboard. MyFigPoint collects the required information (e.g., name, user ID, reward type, and bank account details if necessary). This information is forwarded securely to the brand that issued the points for final verification and payout. The brand processes the reward — which may include product delivery, airtime, or direct payment — within 3–7 working days.

Preferred Reward Types
Product Rewards

Herbal products, merchandise, gifts.

Digital Rewards

Airtime, data bundles, e-vouchers.

Service Rewards

Discounts, event access, training.

Cash-Equivalent

Monetary value (if permitted).

Note: The availability of specific reward types depends on each participating company’s policy and discretion.

4. Role of MyFigPoint

Acts as a facilitator that bridges users and companies. Provides the digital infrastructure for tracking and managing points. Does not process payments or store user funds. Once a request is successfully forwarded to a brand, MyFigPoint’s role in the payout process is complete.

5. Partner Responsibility

Each participating brand is fully responsible for verifying, approving, and completing its reward payouts. All forms of payment or physical reward delivery are done by the respective company, not by MyFigPoint.
